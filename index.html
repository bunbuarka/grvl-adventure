About Us

Hi! We are a couple who lives in Tokyo city for weekdays but mostly outside of the city,
unplug ourselves with hectic ness, exploring and recharge on weekend!

If you love #outdoor #adventure #adrenalinerush,
join our trip here and let’s recharge together in the wilderness!!

Cycling Tour
It is like a La La Land for cyclist here in Japan!

Any types of terrains you could choose, from roads in the city,
gravel along the river, never ending bridges from an island to another island,
hills with amazing mountain view, and many more!

If you happen to come around May - October, it is the best season for Bike Camping!
Only cycling around 50 km from Tokyo along the river,
we could find camping places here and there at the very edge of Tokyo surrounding with forrest and rivers.

Camping Trip
Experience Japan's camping environment unlike any other place in the world!

whether it's Glamping, Auto Camping, Bike Camping,

enjoy the camp under the stars

just next to the river

And don’t worry! In Japan, you need nothing to do camping,
because they rent everything at the camping site along with hot shower

Mountain Adventure
You will never get bored in the mountain area of Japan!

Climb the famous Mt. Fuji,

Explore the beauty of Yakushima Island,

or just a day trip to Mt. Takao, Tokyo's most visited mountain

Every season, different views! You won’t get bored even though you go the same mountains again and again

Winter Sport

Who said winter is about staying at home or the hotel?
If you are an adrenaline junkie, you will find the awesomeness with winter sport!
